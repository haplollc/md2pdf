# Why This Version Works So Well

![A quiet European city street](https://picsum.photos/seed/europe-street/700/260)

This itinerary gives you:
- iconic cities
- Alps
- lakes
- beaches
- islands
- historic architecture
- slower pacing

while avoiding:
- border stress
- exhausting transfer days
- overpacked schedules

The environments constantly evolve:
Amsterdam canals → Paris cafés → Swiss Alps → Austrian mountain city → Slovenian lakes → Adriatic islands

which helps the trip feel exciting the entire way through.
